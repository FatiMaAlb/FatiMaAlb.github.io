My Projects

Tawtheeq platform

I contributed as part of a development team in building a platform for documenting courses and training programs at Imam Abdulrahman bin Faisal University. The system enables trainers to create and automatically send certificates, while allowing trainees to access and manage all their certificates through a personal account.

HeadRest: Migraine Tracking System

The HeadRest system is an optimal solution that helps users monitor their migraines and follow up with their doctors. Based on the research we conducted, It is clear that developing an accurate system to help people monitor headaches will be a successful system and improve the quality of life for a large segment of society.

CNN Models for Facial Emotion Recognition

A convolutional neural network (CNN) developed for facial emotion recognition. The model was initially trained on the FER2013 dataset, achieving up to 70% accuracy. Different optimizers and hyperparameter configurations were explored to enhance performance. To ensure robustness and generalizability, the model was further evaluated on multiple benchmark datasets, including CK, RAF-DB, AffectNet, JAFFE, FEMI-DB, and EmoRec-DB.
